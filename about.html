About Southwood Automotive

Your trusted partner for automotive excellence in South Calgary

Our Story

Founded over 15 years ago, Southwood Automotive Ltd has been serving the South Calgary community with honesty, integrity, and exceptional automotive service.

What started as a small neighborhood shop has grown into one of Calgary's most trusted auto repair facilities, thanks to our unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

We believe that honest, transparent service shouldn't be the exception in the auto industry — it should be the standard. That's why we've built our entire business around treating every customer like family and every vehicle like our own.
